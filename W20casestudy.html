Stayfresh

StayFresh is an app to manage food inventory, keeping things fresh by notifying users before it turns bad. Easy recipes based on available ingredients are suggested to save time on deciding what to cook. Users can see how much food and money they wasted each month so they can aim for 0 food waste.

Research

Interviews

Interviews were conducted to determine why people throw things away, and what was being thrown out.

Surveys

The team sent out surveys to collect additional data in addition to interviews.

The problem?

Busy individuals need access to quick recipes to use up perishable items in their household, and an accessible food inventory list with a focus on expiration.
